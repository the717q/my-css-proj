Why i became a web developer

For my own part, i have been unconsistent and have been working in several areas and countries almost all my 20's, and it came the time when i thought the best is to settle down and focus on a career so i can have more stability and grow on a specific niche. I choose web development because i have a background with C++ and it is easier for me to learn a new language.

Focusing on Programing fundamentals i started a course and learning Javascript by scratch. Since front end development involves more than Javascript, the best choice is to learn as i keep working on new projects that require multiple tasks.
